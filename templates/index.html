    • Why Take the BDI Quiz?

    The BDI (Beck Depression Inventory) Quiz is designed to help you understand your emotional and cognitive well-being. By taking these quizzes, you can gain insights into different aspects of your mental health.

    How to Use the BDI Quiz?

    Simply click on Start to begin. Quiz focuses on a specific area - affective, cognitive, or somatic. Answer the questions honestly to get the most accurate results.

    Good Being!

    Remember, taking care of your mental health is important. Regardless of the results, use this quiz as a tool for self-awareness and seek professional help if needed.
